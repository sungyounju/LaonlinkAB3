Comprehensive Industrial Automation Solutions

Our carefully curated product catalog covers the full spectrum of industrial automation and control systems, providing European manufacturers with one reliable source for their component needs.

Programmable Logic Controllers (PLCs)

Our PLC selection features the renowned Mitsubishi MELSEC series, including Q-Series, iQ-R, and FX models that deliver reliable control solutions for manufacturing automation. From compact micro PLCs suitable for simple machine control to high-performance modular systems capable of managing complex factory automation networks, we provide controllers for applications ranging from basic on-off control to sophisticated motion control and data acquisition systems. Available models include CPU units, I/O modules, communication modules, and specialty modules for analog control and positioning.

Servo Motors & Drive Systems

Precision motion control is critical for modern manufacturing, and our inventory includes AC servo motors, servo amplifiers, and complete drive systems from leading manufacturers. Available in power ranges from 50W to 15kW, our servo systems are suitable for CNC machines, robotics, automated assembly equipment, and precision packaging machinery. We stock both current-production models and legacy components, ensuring you can maintain and upgrade existing equipment while enjoying access to the latest servo technology for new installations.

Human-Machine Interfaces (HMI) & Display Panels

Effective operator control starts with intuitive interfaces. Our HMI and touchscreen panels provide the visual gateway between your operators and automated systems. From basic text displays to high-resolution color touchscreens with advanced graphics capabilities, we supply panels compatible with all major PLC brands and communication protocols including Ethernet/IP, PROFINET, and CC-Link.

Inverters & Variable Frequency Drives

Control motor speed and optimize energy consumption with our selection of AC inverters and variable frequency drives. Suitable for pumps, fans, conveyors, and general machinery applications, our inverter inventory includes single-phase and three-phase models from 0.2kW to 30kW with advanced features like vector control, energy-saving modes, and built-in communication capabilities.

Motion Control & Linear Systems

Precision linear motion is essential for many automation applications. We stock THK ball screws, linear guides, actuators, and stepping motor systems that deliver accurate positioning and smooth motion. Whether you're building custom machinery or maintaining existing equipment, our motion control components meet the demanding requirements of industrial applications.

Sensors & Detection Systems

Reliable sensing is the foundation of automated control. Our sensor inventory includes proximity sensors, photoelectric sensors, pressure transducers, temperature controllers, and specialty sensors for specific industrial applications. We supply sensors compatible with standard industrial voltage levels and output types, ensuring easy integration with your existing control systems.
