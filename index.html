Europe's Premier Industrial Automation Components Supplier

LaonLinkAB (laon2link) is your trusted partner for high-quality industrial automation components in Sweden and across Europe. We specialize in supplying premium semiconductor components, programmable logic controllers (PLCs), servo systems, and precision automation parts to manufacturers, system integrators, and industrial facilities throughout the region.

With over 3,900+ products in stock, we offer an extensive catalog featuring leading brands including Mitsubishi Electric, THK, Panasonic, MISUMI, and many more. Our inventory includes Mitsubishi MELSEC-Q series PLCs, servo motors and drivers, ball screws, stepping motors, temperature controllers, sensors, cables, power supplies (SMPS), reducers, and a wide range of electrical components for factory automation.

Based in Arlöv, Sweden, we provide competitive EUR pricing, expert technical support, fast delivery across Europe, and genuine OEM components. Whether you're upgrading existing systems, replacing faulty components, or building new automation solutions, our team of experienced engineers is ready to assist with product selection, compatibility questions, and technical specifications.
